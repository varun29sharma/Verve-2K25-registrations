Ignite the Stage. Unleash Your Energy.

                
           

**VERVE 2K25** is the official annual **freshers' event at Vasavi College of Engineering**, exclusively for first-year students.

           

Organized by last year's title holders, Verve 2025 will feature a variety of competitions where the most enthusiastic first-years will compete for several titles.

           

First-year students can participate in one or more events, but only the best performers will be honored with their respective titles.

           

EVENT DATES & TIMINGS:

           

Verve 2025 will take place from **October 28th to November 4th**, between 4:30 PM and 6:30 PM each day. Additional buses will be available at **6:30 PM** throughout the week.
